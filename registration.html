Registration


We welcome your online registration for IEEE ISICAS 2025.


  • At least one author of each accepted paper must register for the conference at a full registration rate no later than August 30, 2025 in order for the author's paper to be presented at the conference and included in the journals.
  • Each paper must be covered by a FULL registration fee.
  • Student Registration fees do NOT ensure paper publication.
  • Each Author Registration is valid for ONE paper submission.

You need to create an ACCOUNT to register.

YOUR ATTENTION: this account is different from the Account used for the submission.

If you need to register several persons, please create an account for each of them.

1 Account = 1 Registration = 1 single Email address.

Each paper must be covered by a FULL registration fee.

Student Registration fees do NOT ensure paper publication.

Each full registration fee can cover 1 paper.
