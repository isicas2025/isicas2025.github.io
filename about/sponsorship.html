About IEEE International Symposium on Integrated Circuits and Systems (ISICAS)

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) Circuits and System (CAS) Society is one of the largest society in IEEE with more than 20K members, including more than 8K student members. The Society has a strong global presence through 68 chapters, 59 joint chapters, and 124 student branch chapters. It supports a rich body of knowledge through 19 journals and fosters collaboration and innovation through 9 flagship and 34 co-sponsored conferences.
Among all, the IEEE International Symposium on Integrated Circuits and Systems (ISICAS) is the top perimer flagship conference of the IEEE CAS Society. ISICAS invites high-quality submissions presenting original work in the areas of integrated circuits and systems. Topics are in the areas of analog, digital, power, energy, biomedical, sensor interfaces, and communication systems.
The submissions must describe integrated circuit and system implementations with experimental results. All accepted papers will be published in special issues of our IEEE CAS flagship journals, such as TCAS-I, TCAS-II, TVLSI, TBioCAS, TCASAI, and OJCAS. Extended versions of recent conference papers already published with significant additional material are welcome.

The ISICAS 2025 will be held in Qingdao, Shandong, China from 24-26 October 2025

  • Theme: “Advances in Integrated Circuits and Systems: Bridging the Gap between Design and Implementation” – fully covers the field of interest of CAS Society.
  • Experienced, regional balanced, and gender diverse team from academia and industry with strong support from government and local companies.
  • There are about thousand people around the world will attend the conference, including students, professors, industry professionals, and government officials.
  • The conference contains most areas of integrated circuits and systems. The topics include analog, digital, power, energy, biomedical, sensor interfaces, AI, and communication systems.
