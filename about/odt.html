One Day Trip

Route1: Traveling in Qingdao with a century old history

Time Trip Recommendation
08:00-09:00 morning call & Enjoy breakfast in the hotel
09:30-10:00 Take a bus from Haitian Hotel to the scenic spot
10:00-10:40 Visit Qingdao Welcome Hotel (formerly the official residence of the German Governor General's Office)
and admire typical architecture from the Wilhelmian period in Germany
10:40-10:50 Walking to scenic spots
10:50-11:40 Visit Xiaoyushan Park, a mountain park in the urban area, and overlook the beautiful Qianhai scenery of red tiles,
green trees, blue sea, and blue sky in the old city area from a high altitude
11:40-12:00 Take the car to the dining restaurant
12:00-13:30 Enjoy lunch
13:30-13:50 Take a car to the scenic spot
13:50-14:50 Visit the Qingdao Beer Museum, watch the century old Qingdao factory and production line,
and taste the freshest raw beer.
14:50-15:10 Take a car to Zhongshan Road
15:10-16:10 Visit the century old street and see it revitalize with new vitality
17:10-18:30 Return to the hotel by car


Route2: The Foremost Coastal Mountain

Time Trip Recommendation
08:00-09:00 Morning call & Breakfast at the hotel
09:00-09:30/td> Transfer to Mount Lao Scenic Area (renowned as "The Foremost Coastal Mountain")
09:30-09:55 Take eco-friendly shuttle bus to Taiqing Palace
09:45-10:45 Have a visit of the ancestral temple of Taoism in Laoshan and the largest Taoist temple in the area
10:45-11:00 Take shuttle bus back to the entrance
11:00-11:10 Transfer to the restaurant
11:10-12:30 Lunch at Mount Lao Academy
12:30-13:30 Health-preserving Baduanjin (Eight-Section Brocade) exercise
13:30-14:30 Traditional Chinese tea ceremony experience
14:30-15:30 DIY mugwort herbal waist pillow workshop
