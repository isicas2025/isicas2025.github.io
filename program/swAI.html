IEEE Standards Workshop on AI for Healthcare

The 3rd IEEE Standards Workshop on AI for Healthcare

The Third IEEE Standards Workshop on AI for Healthcare, in collaboration with the IEEE International Symposium on Integrated Circuits and Systems, presents an exceptional opportunity that seamlessly bridges cutting-edge technology and medical expertise. In this era of rapid advancements in AI, comprehending the vital role of international standards in ensuring the secure, ethical, and impactful implementation of AI technologies is crucial for all stakeholders. This workshop serves as a unique platform for fostering interdisciplinary collaboration, bringing together esteemed medical doctors, engineers, researchers, and industry leaders. Our distinguished panel of medical experts, AI researchers, and standardization specialists are eager to guide you through the intricate landscape of AI in healthcare. Engage in captivating sessions, interactive discussions, and practical case studies to gain valuable insights into how international standards empower data privacy, interoperability, and patient-centric care. Join us and unlock the transformative potential of AI in healthcare while upholding the highest standards of quality and ethics.


Date
