IEEE CASS/ISICAS Beijing Seasonal School

Introduction

The IEEE International Symposium on Integrated Circuits and Systems (IEEE ISICAS), supported by the IEEE Circuits and Systems Society, is organizing Seasonal Schools to engage students, early-career researchers, and practitioners in Integrated Circuits and Signal Processing.

Beijing Seasonal School distinguishes itself as a hub for cutting-edge academic exchange, capitalizing on Beijing’s position as China’s center for higher education, national laboratories, and frontier research. With strong links between academia and technology development, the school creates a focused environment for participants to engage in high-level discussions and collaboratively explore emerging solutions in integrated circuits (IC) and advanced computing systems. The Beijing Seasonal School will explore forward-looking areas such as next-generation memory technologies, efficient AI-driven hardware, real-time heterogeneous computing, and innovative VLSI design approaches, aiming to equip participants with the vision and skills to tackle future challenges in both research and industry.
