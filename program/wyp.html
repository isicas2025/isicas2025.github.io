Joint WiCAS-YP Event @ ISICAS 2025

“Women in Circuits and Systems” (WiCAS) and “Young Professionals” (YP) event aims to inspire and motivate both students and young professionals in the domain of circuits and systems to have efficient roles in their professions.

Joining this WiCAS & YP event, you will have the opportunity to learn about state of the art and most advanced activities, meet and interact with your peers, receive feedback from experts from both academia and industry. Don’t miss the chance to attend!


WICAS & YP EVENT -26TH oCTOBER 2025 15:20-17:00

Theme: “Beyond Al: Reshaping Your Career in the Al-Powered Era”


15:20~15:25 Welcome Speech (5 mins)
15:25~16:25 Speakers from Academia and Industry (60 mins)
16:25~16:55 Roundtable talk (30 mins)
