CASS Student Travel Grants

The IEEE Circuits and Systems Society (CASS) offers Student Travel Support to enable IEEE CASS Student Members to present their research at flagship CASS conferences. Full details and guidelines are available on the CASS Website
https://ieee-cas.org/post/news/2025-ieee-cass-flagship-student-travel-grants-round-5-final-round The submission deadline for the 2025 Student Travel Support Round 5 is September 4, 2025. Please note that each conference will only be included in one Round, so you must apply during that relevant period.
Nominations will be reviewed confidentially by the ISICAS 2025 Evaluation Committees and the CASS Operations Office. They will be kept confidential. The grants will be provided as a reimbursement to expenses for travel to the conference only, and can not be reimbursed for conference registration. The reimbursements will be handled by CASS via Concur. Please note that the final amount given to the awardee will be based on expenses incurred by the students to attend the conference, not to exceed the awarded amount.

Selection Criteria

Student(s) receiving these grants must have had their papers selected for presentation at ISICAS 2025.

  1. A faculty member must confirm that the nominee:
    1. is an undergraduate or graduate student and
    2. is an IEEE CASS student member.
